About This Project

Our Project

This website is an academic project created for the course, "Community Engagement Project: Digital Empowerment: Building Skills for a Connected Community."

Our goal is to explore and highlight the tangible results of community engagement programs by showcasing the work of five dedicated non-governmental organizations:

  • SankalpTaru Foundation
  • Indian Red Cross Society
  • Pratham
  • Akshaya Patra Foundation
  • Water Aid India

Through this site, we aim to bring awareness to the profound and measurable impact these organizations have on our community.

The Team

We are a team of Second Year B.Tech Computer Engineering students (Division C) from Progressive Education Society's Modern College of Engineering.
