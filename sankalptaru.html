SankalpTaru Foundation

Our Mission

At SankalpTaru, we use technological and ecological expertise to fight climate change, creating a greener planet by the people, for the people.

Our Vision

SankalpTaru envisions a sustainable future through a people-powered, tech-driven approach to environmental conservation.

Key Focus Areas

  • Afforestation and ecosystem restoration
  • Water conservation
  • Community development
  • Rural livelihood support
  • Nutrition programs
  • Technology integration

Our Story & Approach

An IT-Enabled Green Movement

SankalpTaru Foundation is an environmentally-focused NGO that skillfully blends technology with conservation. Their initiatives go beyond just planting trees; they encompass afforestation, ecosystem restoration, water conservation projects, community awareness sessions, and nutrition programs, all aimed at combating climate change and fostering sustainable communities.

As an official actor of the UN Decade on Ecosystem Restoration, SankalpTaru is committed to achieving the UN SDG Goals by 2030, aligning its programs with global sustainability objectives.

Our Operational Models

  • Rural Livelihood Support Program

    Boosts rural economies, health, and education. We are growing Organic Apple Villages in the cold desert of Ladakh to support farmer livelihoods.

  • Community Barren Land Transformation Program

    Transforms barren and wastelands into biodiverse forests. We are growing large forests on the ecologically fragile Aravallis near Delhi.

  • Clean and Green School Program

    Creates green campuses to cultivate eco-conscious citizens, teaching students how science and nature work together.

  • Urban Plantation Program

    Enhances urban green spaces to improve air quality and community well-being, in collaboration with partners like DCB Bank.

  • Mangrove Restoration Program

    Restores coastal ecosystems by planting mangroves, protecting shorelines, and supporting marine life in areas like the Sundarbans.

  • Drone Seed Bombing

    Utilizes cutting-edge drone technology to plant seeds in challenging areas, such as landslide-affected regions of Uttarakhand in partnership with Flipkart.

Our Scale & Commitment

To date, SankalpTaru Foundation has successfully planted over 10 million trees across India, transforming landscapes and empowering rural communities.
